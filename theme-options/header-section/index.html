Header Related Options

This section has multiple self explaining settings as listed below.

1 - You can choose header style of your choice form given three variations.

2 - You can enable/disable the sticky header option.

3 - You can show/hide the site pre-loader.  

 

Header Social Icons

In Social Links sub menu, you can enable disable header social links form the section.

You can follow the guided format in that option description to add social links in header. The format is also given below.

icon,http://social-link.com

To see full link of icons you can visit the fontawesome icon page
