Measurement Widget

This widget shows information regarding the measurements related to the recipes being displayed on this website.

1 - You can set the title for this widget.

2 - You can display the measurements on your website according to your needs. The example measurements are given below.

1 cup = 250 ml = 16 Tablespoons 1/2 cup = 125 ml = 8 Tablespoons 1/3 cup = 83 ml = 5.3 Tablespoons 1/4 cup = 62 ml = 4 Tablespoons 1 Pinch = 1/8 Teaspoon
